# Emberwick — GM EYES ONLY

> **Firewall**: Narrator must not read this file.

## Hidden facts about the place

- The hill shrine is a **warding site**: the ever-burning lantern is a minor
  enchantment that keeps whatever nests beneath the stones dormant. The
  builders are long gone; nobody in Emberwick knows the lantern *does*
  anything — it's just tradition.
- With the lantern dark, the nest under the shrine (giant centipedes around a
  cracked warding stone — reskin or rescale freely) is waking. The missing
  sheep are its first meals.
- **Piven the lamplighter is alive**, holed up in the shrine cellar with a
  sprained ankle and a dwindling sack of oat biscuits. He cracked the lantern
  housing while cleaning it; he heard things moving under the floor and hid.
